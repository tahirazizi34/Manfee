Afghan Card Game — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

T-Teka ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to run the game:

  • Username — chosen by you at signup, displayed to other players
  • Password — stored as a one-way cryptographic hash (we cannot read it)
  • Game statistics — credits, level, games played, wins
  • Game replays — stored locally on your device for up to 24 hours

We do not collect: real names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your game account
  • To display your username and stats to other players during games
  • To maintain leaderboard rankings
  • To save your game progress and credits

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your account data is stored on our secure game server. Game replays are stored only on your device's local storage and automatically deleted after 24 hours. We do not back up replay data to our servers.

4. Online Multiplayer

When playing online, your username and in-game actions (bids, cards played) are transmitted to other players in your game room in real time. Voice chat audio, if enabled, is transmitted directly between players (peer-to-peer) and is not recorded or stored.

5. Children's Privacy

T-Teka is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 with a server-side salt. Authentication tokens are signed and expire. We use HTTPS for all data transmission.

7. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Local replay data can be cleared by clearing your browser or app cache.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
